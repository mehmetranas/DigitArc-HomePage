We Are DigitArc

We Are Happy to Assist
You all Time Moment

From understanding your business to a tailored solution, we help you in every step. Based on our experience from 50+ projects for clients on enterprise level such as Daimler AG, Siemens AG and thyssenkrupp AG and various departments from Finance, R&D to Logistics, we are able to provide high quality Software Solutions for your approaches.
  • How do you start with a new project?

    Independent of having an existing software solution or beginning from greenfield, we analyse at first what the target of your software is and check who the users are. The results are giving us the possibility to design an UI/UX to make it easy to use for the users and provide the requirements of the business.

  • Do you use a specific software development workflow?

    We are developing preferred in an Agile way in most cases Scrum. The benefits of this is that our client is able to see the progress of the development in our sprint board (Azure DevOps / Jira).

  • Which technologies do you use?

    Our preferred technologies are the Microsoft Stack: .NET 5 (formerly .NET Core / .NET), Azure Cloud, ML.NET and Frontend Frameworks like ReactJs, React Native, Angular, Ionic, Vue. Bu we are also using Pyhton for Data Analytics and Typescript for a better maintainable Frontend.

  • Are you experienced in Enterprise Software Architecture and Design Patterns?

    Of course! We love Eric Evans Domain Driven Design Development Strategies and follow also Martin Fowler to extend our knowledge. In concrete scenarios we implemented a Microservice Architecture with Serverless Functions, Event Sourcing and CQRS to provide a maximum advantage to our client. For more details don't hesitate to contact us.

  • Do you provide support after the Software Development is completed?

    Yes! We give you the guarantee if you find any bugs which are related to our written code we fix it free of charge. We also provide an enterprise support for our Software.

  • How you can guarantee high quality code?

    First of all, we are a team of senior developers with at least +6 years experience in global companies. But fairly it is not enough, therefore we work with Git flow Pull Request where the code is double checked before merged in a deployable branch. Within our CI/CD Pipelines we check a test coverage of at least 80% and also check the score of the code quality with Sonarqube.
